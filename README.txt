CLEARWAVETX LIVE TRANSCRIBER
=============================


AUDIO SETUP:
1. Ensure audio input is connected to the Focusrite
2. Connect the Focusrite to the MS01 device via USB-C
3. Test audio levels before recording


RUNNING THE TRANSCRIBER:
1. Open any terminal window (PowerShell or Command Prompt)
2. Type: transcribe
3. Press Enter

You will be prompted:

   Enter the filename for your transcription (e.g. meeting_notes):

Enter a filename or press Enter to auto-generate one with a timestamp.

The app will then load and begin recording. You will see:

   [INFO] Recording started. Press 'q' to stop.

4. Start sending audio to the Focusrite
5. The app will process audio every 45 seconds and display the transcribed text


STOPPING THE TRANSCRIBER:
- Press Ctrl + C  (recommended)
- Or type 'q' and press Enter

The transcription will be saved automatically when you stop.


SAVED FILES:
Transcripts are saved to: C:\Users\compu\Desktop\live-transcriber
Each file begins with "Transcription by ClearwaveTX"
